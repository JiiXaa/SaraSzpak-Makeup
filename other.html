Makeup Application Services

I am available for all your makeup artistry needs. From one-on-one makeup lessons, a photoshoot/videoshoot, your birthday, prom, graduation, to your wedding day, all special occasions, or even to get dolled up for a great night out. I am involved with every client I work with, and I enjoy making long-term relationships with all of my clients. Know that you will have a fantastic experience, and I will do whatever I can to achieve your perfect look for whatever the occasion may be.

A Night Out | Special Occasion Makeup

(Time is approximately 1 hour)

Do you have a special occasion, an event, a night of fun in the city, or attending a party? Then, get dressed in your best and leave the makeup to me! I do travel to my clients to help them get ready in the comfort of their own homes or hotel. Makeup application includes full face, contouring, eyes, cheeks, lips, brow sculpting, and lashes.

(Time is approximately 1 hour)

Photoshoots | Engagement | Maternity | Glamour

(Time is approximately 1 hour)

Photoshoot makeup is a complete long-wearing and shine-free makeup application with eyelashes that enhance your natural beauty and translate flawlessly on camera. Makeup applications can be in your home, hotel or on-location.
