Bridal Makeup Services

Your wedding day is one of the most memorable days of your life. The spotlight is on you that day for the bride, and you should feel and look your best. I am here to help you do just that. Bridal trials are available beforehand, where we can work together to achieve your perfect wedding day look. I will execute the makeup look we've decided on your wedding day. In addition, I am available to stay around for the duration of your wedding if needed for touchups or a look change.

Bridal Makeup | Bridal Hairstyle

(Time is approximately 1 hour for the bride, 40 minutes per bridal party member)

Luxury full-face bridal makeup application with high-quality skin and makeup products complete with eyelashes. Bridal makeup applications are long-lasting and made to photograph well. Traditional and airbrush makeup is available. Providing makeup services for the bride, bridesmaids, bridal party, family members, and attendants.

Bridal Trial Makeup/Hairstyle

(Time is approximately 1 hour to 2 hours)

A bridal trial is a preview of your wedding day makeup look. You will get to meet your makeup artist and get to know my personality and talents. We can discuss your ideas and we will work together to create and achieve your perfect wedding day look. We will figure out what products work best for your skin type, what makeup styles suit you best, and what makes you feel most confident. Traditional and airbrush makeup is available and the choice of individual or strip false eyelashes. I will execute the makeup look we've perfected on your wedding day.

Engagement Party | Bridal Shower

(Time is approximately 1 hour)

Celebrating your engagement or upcoming wedding festivities? Get dressed in your best and leave the makeup to me! I can travel to my brides-to-be to help them get ready in the comfort of their own homes, hotel, or take in-studio appointments. Makeup application includes full face, contouring, eyes, cheeks, lips, brow sculpting, and lashes.

Photoshoots | Makeup For Engagement | Boudoir

(Time is approximately 1 hour)

Photoshoot makeup is a complete long-wearing and shine-free makeup application with eyelashes that enhance your natural beauty and translate flawlessly on camera. Makeup applications can be in-studio or on-location.
