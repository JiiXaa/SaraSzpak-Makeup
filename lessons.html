Makeover | Makeup Lessons | Makeup Classes

(Time is approximately 1 1/2 to 2 hours)

Makeovers and makeup lessons are available to those who want a new signature look or a basic rundown on applying makeup. I can help you go through your cosmetic bag and teach you the basics, tips, techniques, and tricks. You can show me any pictures of looks you would like to achieve. I customize all lessons based on what you want to accomplish and learn. When the lesson is completed, you will receive detailed face charts of your completed looks for reference, printed directions on how to achieve the makeup look, and a personalized product list of recommendations.

Beauty Consulting | Personal Shopping

(Time is approximately 1 1/2 to 2 hours)

I can go through your makeup bag, vanity, or train case and help you decide what to keep and purge. We can identify what beauty dilemmas you have and how to accommodate them. It can be overwhelming to walk into a store or beauty counter and not know what to pick out for yourself. Through Personal Shopping, I can help eliminate that frustration or confusion by either shopping with you or for you. I'll find the best products that fit your needs, lifestyle, and budget.
