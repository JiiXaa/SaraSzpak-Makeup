Occasion Hair & Makeup

Whether you’re attending a special event, celebrating a milestone, preparing for a photoshoot or getting ready for prom, professional hair and makeup can help you look and feel your absolute best.

Every look is tailored to suit your personal style, features and the occasion itself.

From soft, natural beauty to full glamour, my aim is to create a polished, long-lasting look that photographs beautifully and gives you confidence throughout your event. Services are available for proms, photoshoots, birthdays, engagement celebrations, special events and other occasions.

Hair and makeup can be booked individually or together and are available on-location throughout South Wales and surrounding areas.
