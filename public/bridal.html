Bridal Preview

(Please allow up to 3 hours for your bridal preview appointment.)

A bridal preview is the perfect opportunity to refine and perfect both your hair and makeup ahead of your wedding day.


During your appointment, we will explore your vision, wedding style and inspiration to create a look that feels authentically you. Together, we will perfect every detail, ensuring your chosen hairstyle and makeup complement your features, dress and overall bridal aesthetic beautifully.


Bridal previews are recommended approximately 6–8 weeks before your wedding date and are designed to give you complete confidence ahead of your special day. You’re welcome to bring your veil, hair accessories and inspiration images to help bring your vision to life.
